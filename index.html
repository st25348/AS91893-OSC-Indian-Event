Our Mission

Our mission is to celebrate the richness of Indian culture through authentic cuisine, traditional games, and engaging activities, while bringing the community together. This event not only introduces guests to India’s vibrant heritage but also supports a meaningful charitable cause, with all proceeds going to help those in need. By combining culture, food, and charity, we aim to inspire learning, appreciation, and community involvement in a fun and memorable way.

What's On

Experience the Event

Games & Activities

Take part in a selection of traditional Indian games designed to encourage participation, enjoyment, and cultural learning. These activities create an engaging environment where guests of all ages can connect and have fun.

Storytelling

Experience the richness of Indian culture through interactive storytelling sessions. These moments bring traditional folktales to life, creating an engaging and meaningful experience for all visitors.

Charity Support

Support a meaningful cause while enjoying the event. All proceeds contribute to the Starship Foundation, helping provide essential healthcare and support for children and families in need.

Dine-In Experience

Enjoy a premium late-evening dining experience featuring authentic Indian cuisine prepared by students. Guests can relax and immerse themselves in a warm, cultural atmosphere centred around food and community.
