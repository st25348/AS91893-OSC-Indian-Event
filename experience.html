Experience the Event

Relive the unforgettable moments from our OSC Indian Cultural Event through our exclusive gallery.

A Night of Good Food

Families and friends gathered to share authentic Indian cuisine, freshly prepared by our talented student chefs.

Made by Our Students

From the kitchen to the stage, every part of this evening was brought to life by the students of Ormiston Senior College.

In the Kitchen

Every dish is thoughtfully prepared with care and attention, ensuring a tidy and hygienic kitchen while students learn authentic Indian cooking techniques.

Fresh Ingredients

Every dish starts with fresh, high-quality vegetables and ingredients, ensuring authentic flavors and vibrant colors on your plate.

Made with Love

Prepared with care and passion, our students put love into every dish, creating meals that celebrate Indian culinary traditions.

Health-Conscious Cooking

Attention to hygiene and health-conscious cooking makes each dish both delicious and nourishing for everyone to enjoy.

Experience

Guests are welcomed into a vibrant atmosphere where food, culture, and community come together. The event allows visitors to enjoy authentic Indian dishes while experiencing the warmth and hospitality that are central to Indian traditions.

Student Participation

Students play an important role in organising and running the event. From preparing dishes to guiding activities and helping guests, their involvement creates a collaborative environment that builds teamwork and leadership skills.

Support a Cause While Enjoying Indian Culture

The event brings the school community together while supporting a meaningful cause. Funds raised during the event contribute to the Starship Foundation, helping improve healthcare and support for children and families.
